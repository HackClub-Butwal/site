About Hack Club Butwal

Hack Club Butwal is a student-led coding club affiliated with the international Hack Club network. We're on a mission to foster coding culture among high school and college students in Butwal, Nepal.

Through workshops, events, and community activities, we empower students to build projects, learn new technologies, and collaborate with like-minded peers.

Join us to learn, build, and share your creative ideas in a supportive community!
